About Selling Put Options

When you sell a put option:

  • You receive premium upfront (income)
  • You agree to buy shares at the strike price if assigned
  • Each contract represents 100 shares
  • If the stock stays above the strike price, the option expires worthless and you keep the premium
Risk Warning: Selling put options obligates you to purchase shares at the strike price if assigned. Make sure you have sufficient funds available.
